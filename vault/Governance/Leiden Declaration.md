# Leiden Declaration

The governance section will distinguish four questions:

1. who defines worthwhile mathematical problems;
2. who controls compute and access;
3. who receives attribution and bears responsibility;
4. which artifacts and evaluation traces are open to scrutiny.

## Operational summary

- **Open:** arguments and infrastructure should remain inspectable.
- **Disclose:** report tools, compute, and meaningful AI intervention.
- **Own:** humans take credit and responsibility for the claim.
