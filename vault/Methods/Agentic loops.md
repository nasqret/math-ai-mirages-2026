# Agentic loops

Canonical loop:

plan → execute → test → critique → checkpoint

Every iteration must leave:

1. a state;
2. a test;
3. a reason to continue or stop.

Budgets, retries, tools, human interventions, and output selection are part of
the method. A one-shot answer and a 1,000-call stateful campaign are different
experimental treatments.

Common failure: optimizing the wrong representation for longer.
