# Narrative spine

1. The headlines are real data, but not yet a theory of capability.
2. Verification changes the economics of mathematical search.
3. Agents and loops turn a model into a research process.
4. Case studies show genuine leverage and genuine brittleness.
5. Benchmarks measure systems under protocols, not disembodied intelligence.
6. Governance must remain with mathematicians and public institutions.
7. Near-term forecast: rapid completion and formalization; slow emergence of
   genuinely new mathematical structure.
