# Quartic lines

Joint work with Piotr Pokora. Exact characteristic-zero quartic:

$$
x_0^4-x_0x_1^3-x_2^4+x_2x_3^3=0.
$$

Profile and certificate:

$$
(n,t_2,t_3,t_4)=(24,0,32,0),\quad
\bar c_1^2=112,\quad \bar c_2=40,
$$

$$
\frac{\bar c_1^2}{\bar c_2}=\frac{14}{5}>\frac83,
\qquad G=10n-2t_2-t_3=208.
$$

Finite-field search was followed by exact reconstruction and independent
SageMath/Magma verification. The model label is provenance; the certificate is
the evidence.
