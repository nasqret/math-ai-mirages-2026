# One-to-two-year forecast

Working hypothesis for verification:

- agents will become substantially better at completing, checking, and
  translating research within established representational regimes;
- autoformalization will improve faster than autonomous theory formation;
- compute and proprietary access will remain major confounders in comparisons;
- genuinely new durable abstractions and structural viewpoints will remain the
  hardest frontier for LLM-centred architectures.
